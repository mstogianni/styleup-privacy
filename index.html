Privacy Policy for StyleUP

Last updated: September 2025

StyleUP ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the StyleUP mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Email and password (for account creation and login).
  • Photos of clothing or outfits uploaded by you (to generate outfit suggestions).
  • Optional profile details you choose to share.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide app functionality (e.g., outfit suggestions).
  • To secure your account with Firebase Authentication.
  • To improve the app experience.

Permissions

The app may request access to your device's camera and storage to allow you to upload photos of your clothes. These photos remain private and are not shared with third parties.

Data Security

We use Firebase and Google Cloud services to securely store and process your data. We do not sell, rent, or misuse your personal information.
